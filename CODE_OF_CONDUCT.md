# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone involved in the DataShield project will uphold our values of respect, integrity, and inclusivity.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and inclusive
- Welcome diverse perspectives and experiences
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Be patient and understanding

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discrimination
- Offensive comments
- Disruptive behavior
- Plagiarism or intellectual property theft

## Consequences

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in removal from the community or project.

## Reporting

If you witness or experience violations, please report them by opening an issue or contacting a maintainer privately.

---

Thank you for helping make DataShield a positive and productive community!
